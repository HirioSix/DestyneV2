The andes: America stands out for its vast landscapes in countless flora and fauna. Among the places that can stand out we have: The cold Andes mountains dominated by the magnitude of their peaks and a large amount of snow around them. Its name comes from the Quechua language with which the Inca peoples of the region called the geographical east. According to the Inca Garcilaso de la Vega (1539-1616), in the ancient Inca empire it was called "Tahuantinsuyo" which means “the four corners of the Earth”. followed by its surroundings, Patagonia. huge lagoons full of life, flowing rivers and beautiful animals such as pumas, gigantic condors or unmistakable llamas

The green Amazonas: The largest rainforest on the planet covers 5.5 million kilometers. and spans nine countries. Almost twice the size of India The Amazon is the generator of 20% of the oxygen in the Earth's atmosphere A meandering river full of life, amazing animals and one of the largest green places in the world, those are the words that describe the Amazon.
